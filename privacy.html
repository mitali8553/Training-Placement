Privacy Policy

At Training & Placement, we are committed to safeguarding the privacy of students, professionals, and corporate clients who use our Training and Placement services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect personal data through various forms on our website, including:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Educational Qualification
  • Resume or CV (if uploaded)
  • Courses or Services You Are Interested In

Purpose of Data Collection

We use your information for the following legitimate purposes:

  • To respond to your course or placement inquiries
  • To register you for training programs
  • To share relevant job openings and placement opportunities
  • To improve our training modules and services
  • To send notifications or updates regarding training schedules, interviews, or certifications

Data Security

Your information is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized team members. We implement security protocols to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure of your data.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with external agencies for marketing. However, we may share your profile with trusted recruiters or partner companies for placement purposes, only with your consent.

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools to monitor website traffic and enhance user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

Your Consent

By using our website and filling out forms, you consent to our Privacy Policy and terms of service.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or wish to update or delete your personal data, please contact us. We respect your rights and will respond promptly.
